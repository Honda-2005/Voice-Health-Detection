About the Project

Voice Health Detection is an advanced system designed for early disease risk screening using human voice analysis. This platform leverages cutting-edge AI and machine learning techniques to analyze vocal patterns and identify potential health concerns.

Important: This is a risk screening tool, not a diagnostic system. Results should be discussed with healthcare professionals.
